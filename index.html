News

  • I am coteaching a new course EE378C: information-theoretic lower bounds in data science in the spring quarter of 2020-2021 at Stanford. Check out the slides!

  • I start writing an online lecture series of information-theoretic lower bounds. The lecture series will enumerate ideas and tools to establish fundamental limits for problems of different information structures, with lots of concrete examples. Stay tuned!
