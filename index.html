Yanjun Han

 

Assistant Professor of Mathematics, Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences

Assistant Professor of Data Science, Center for Data Science

Assistant Professor of Computer Science, Computer Science Department (affiliated)

New York University

I received my B.E. in Electronic Engineering from Tsinghua University in Jul 2015, and my M.S. and Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Stanford University in Aug 2021, under the supervision of Tsachy Weissman. I was a postdoctoral scholar at the Simons Institute for the Theory of Computing, University of California, Berkeley in 2021-22, and a Norbert Wiener postdoctoral associate at the Statistics and Data Science Center (SDSC) in MIT IDSS.

I am broadly interested in mathematical data science, statistics, online learning and bandits, and information theory.
